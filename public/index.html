How does ShitCoin work?

  • Parimutuel System: All Shit Coins bet on an event go into a single pool.
  • The Payout: When resolved, the entire pool is split among those who bet on the winning option, proportional to their bet.
  • Refunds: If you delete an event, or if an event is resolved with an option that has 0 bets, all wagers are returned.
  • Fair Play: This is a fun, zero-cost fictional currency platform to settle team disputes and make friendly bets!
